# Product Brief

## Problem

Bodybuilding athletes need repeated, specific feedback to improve posing. Most practice happens alone, and mirrors or recorded videos do not provide structured feedback in the moment.

## Solution

PoseAI provides mobile-first pose coaching for bodybuilding practice. The app detects the athlete's body landmarks, evaluates pose-specific metrics, and gives one focused cue at a time.

## Target Users

- Amateur bodybuilding competitors.
- Fitness athletes preparing for stage presentation.
- Coaches who want a lightweight review tool for clients.
- Beginners learning mandatory poses.

## Current Experience

1. Select a bodybuilding pose.
2. Use live camera or import a photo.
3. Let the app detect body landmarks.
4. Receive a score and the highest-priority correction.
5. Save the session and review progress over time.

## Differentiation

- Built specifically around bodybuilding pose structure.
- Uses pose-specific metrics rather than generic movement feedback.
- Keeps analysis on-device during the prototype stage.
- Connects live feedback with progress history.

## Roadmap Ideas

- Side-by-side coach comparison.
- Silhouette-based lat and waist measurements.
- Voice-guided posing rounds.
- Exportable progress reports.
- Coach/client review mode.
- Apple Watch or remote capture support.
